Roasted Squash with Lentils, Ricotta and Basil

This recipe is fragrant and comforting! I love it best in the fall when it's chilly outside. Most of the cooking happens in the oven, which warms up the house and sends nice aromas that reach me from the couch, while I wait for it!

    Ingredients:
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 lb butternut squash
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup green lentils
  • 1 lime
  • 3 cups mushrooms
  • 1 whole milk ricotta
    • For the Basil Oil:
  • 1 cup of basil
  • 3 tbsp lime juice and zest
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

    Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 F
  2. Peel and cut the squash into 2 inch chunks. Add to a sheet pan, drizzle olive oil, season with salt and pepper and toss to coat.
  3. Roast for 15 minutes
  4. Cook lentils according to package. Once they are finished, drain them, add olive oil and lemon zest
  5. Now you're going to pull out the squash, make room in the center for the ricotta, combine the lentils
  6. Once you have all the ingredients arranged, return to oven to roast for about 35 minutes
  7. Lastly, blitz all the ingredients for the basil oil in a food processor. This will be for drizzling on the veggies when served
